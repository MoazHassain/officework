Subjective Outcome Survey

(pre IEP)

Directions:

This survey is “subjective” which means it is based only on your perception and feelings. You don’t have to have specific examples rather we want to know how you are experiencing your child’s educational experience. With this in mind think back over the time we have had your child in our program or since the last IEP meeting and respond to the statements below by Dragging the box or clicking on the line to represent where your experience falls between the two extremes. Honest feedback will help this IEP team focus efforts on supporting your child by surfacing concerns that may otherwise could be difficult to understand or identify.
